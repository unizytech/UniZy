PRESCRIPTION DATA ENTRY FIELDS
===============================

BASIC FIELDS
------------
1. Drug Name/Chemical Name (Search field)
2. Frequency (Checkboxes: M, A, E, N)
3. Duration (Number field)
4. Duration Unit (Dropdown)
5. Quantity (Number field)
6. Intake (Dropdown)
7. Route (Dropdown)
8. Intake Period (Dropdown)
9. Quantity UOM (Dropdown)
10. Instructions (Text area)
11. Investigation (Text area)
12. Next Review Date (Date field)
13. Prescription Valid Upto (Date field)
